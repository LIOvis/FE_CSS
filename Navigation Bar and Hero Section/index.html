About Us


Welcome to Dead Sprinkles Society, where the macabre meets the delicious. We’re not your average ice cream shop—we serve scoops of the strange, the spooky, and the spectacular. Born from a love of gothic aesthetics and sinful sweets, our parlor is a sanctuary for the beautifully bizarre. Whether you're craving a cone as dark as your soul or a sundae that screams elegance, you’ll find your flavor in the shadows. Join the society—membership comes with sprinkles.

Specialty Flavors


Black Sesame Lavender


Earthy black sesame meets ethereal lavender in this beautifully balanced blend of shadow and bloom. Smooth, smoky, and floral, it’s a flavor that lingers like a whispered secret in a candlelit room. Delicate, dark, and utterly enchanting.

Ashes to Ashes


Rich charcoal-infused cream folded with chunks of decadent fudge brownie—this flavor is smoky, sinful, and unapologetically dark. A tribute to sweet endings and darker cravings, Ashes to Ashes tastes like dessert at the end of the world.

Blood Velvet Swirl


A hauntingly rich blend of red velvet and black cherry, tangled in a ribbon of dark chocolate fudge. Decadent, dramatic, and just a little dangerous—this signature swirl is as smooth as velvet and as bold as a midnight vow. One bite, and you’re hooked for eternity.

Funeral Fudge Ripple


A deep, dark chocolate base laced with thick ribbons of bittersweet fudge, Funeral Fudge Ripple is indulgence dressed in mourning. Rich, somber, and satisfyingly dramatic—this flavor is perfect for those who like their sweets with a side of sorrow.

Peanut Butter Pickle Surprise


Sweet, salty, and just a little sinister. Creamy peanut butter collides with the sharp tang of pickled plum in this bold, bewildering blend. It shouldn’t work—but it haunts you in the best way. For the daring souls who crave the unexpected.

Midnight Monarch


A silky blend of blackberry, violet cream, and a hint of anise, this regal scoop is as mysterious as it is mesmerizing. Served in a jet-black waffle cone and dusted with edible silver flakes, Midnight Monarch is decadence in its final form—floral, fruity, and faintly forbidden.
